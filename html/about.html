Konstantin Silich is a Belarusian-American multi-instrumentalist, composer, and songwriter. His original creations run the gamut from straight-up hard rocking tunes to much more esoteric fare. Konstantin's musical poetry - which relies heavily on rhythmic experimentation - is equally matched by his eye for dramatic imagery and his storytelling skill.

His music is often accompanied by intensely visual and thought-provoking videos (directed and produced by him) that challenge the viewer to go deeper, to question our view of accepted reality, and to choose a vision for the future.
