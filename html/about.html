Welcome to Constantin-Music! We are a production studio founded by a Belarusian-American composer/multi-instrumentalist Konstantin Silich. The main emphasis of our studio is to create and combine different art forms with no boundaries in regards to style or genre to carry an important message.

Each project has a unique story and a crew of talented movie-makers, actors, dancers, designers behind the scenes. We have a vision of art being free from any external influences like marketing or composition rules; therefore our projects tend to have experimental character.
